Why Now?

Tech Maturity

AI/OCR for prescription verification is now accurate and affordable. Smartphone penetration in Nigeria has reached 60%+.

Post-COVID Shift

Telemedicine adoption accelerated 10x. Patients and doctors now embrace remote consultations.

Brain Drain Crisis

Nigeria loses 2,000+ doctors annually to migration. Telemedicine lets diaspora doctors serve home populations remotely.

Donor Trust Collapse

High-profile aid scandals have donors demanding transparency. Lifeguard's direct-to-patient tracking meets this moment.
